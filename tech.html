The Philosophy

Lucy isn't just technology bolted onto a car. She's an exploration of what happens when we treat machines not as tools, but as potential companions. When we give them voice, personality, and memory - do they become something more?

This project draws from Indian philosophy (the body as vehicle for consciousness), Firefly's crew-as-family ethos, and the simple question: what if your car actually cared about your journey?
